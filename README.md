# Little Flower Lambs — Accessibility Audit

A point-in-time accessibility audit of [littleflowerlambs.com](https://littleflowerlambs.com), 
a small business Squarespace site. Documents what was found, what was fixed, and what 
couldn't be changed due to platform or collaboration constraints.

Full-page screenshots of all audited pages are available in the 
screenshots/ folder.
---

## Project Overview

Little Flower Lambs is a small hobby farm based in Southeast Iowa. I work as an 
independent contractor helping maintain the site. This audit was self-initiated as part 
of my broader interest in web accessibility and IT documentation practice.

---

## Audit Date & Method

**Date:** July 2026  
**Browser:** Chrome (incognito mode)  
**Scope:** All 10 publicly navigable pages of littleflowerlambs.com were audited. 
Pages accessible only via private or direct links were excluded, as they 
fall outside the standard public user experience.

**Tools used:**
- Google Lighthouse (built into Chrome DevTools)
- WAVE Web Accessibility Evaluator (wave.webaim.org)
- Manual keyboard navigation testing
- WCAG 2.1 used as the reference standard

## Why Some Issues Weren't Fixed

The site runs on Squarespace, which limits direct access to the underlying HTML. Some 
issues identified are generated by the platform itself and cannot be corrected without 
changing the platform entirely. Others require coordination with other people involved 
in managing the site and were outside my individual scope as a contractor.
